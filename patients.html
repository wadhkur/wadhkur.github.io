What your visit typically includes

Pre-visit briefing

Instructions are shared in advance, including any preparation details related to your study.

On-site check-in

Your team reviews safety considerations and confirms that the imaging plan is clear.

Imaging and support

During imaging, communication remains active so you understand each step as it happens.

Post-study discussion

Results pathway and follow-up context are reviewed with your care plan in mind.
