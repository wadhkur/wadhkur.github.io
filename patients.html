For patients

Supportive guidance before, during, and after imaging

You will receive clear instructions, calm pacing during the study, and time to review what the results mean for you.

Visit overview

  • ArrivalPlan to arrive a little early to review any updates and settle in.
  • During imagingThe physician explains each step and checks on your comfort.
  • AfterwardNext steps and timelines are shared in plain language.

Preparing for common studies

Echocardiogram

Wear a comfortable top. You may be asked to change into a gown to allow clear access for ultrasound.

Cardiac CT

Specific instructions may include temporary changes to caffeine or certain medications. Hydration guidance is provided.

Cardiac MRI

Metal screening is completed in advance. Comfort strategies are discussed if you have concerns about enclosed spaces.

Stress imaging

Clothing suited for gentle movement is helpful. The team reviews safety checks before starting.

What to bring

  • Medication listInclude doses and timing so we can coordinate safely.
  • Allergy detailsShare any history of contrast or medication reactions.
  • Prior imagingDigital copies help with comparison when available.

After your study

Results are communicated in clear language. Questions are welcomed, and summaries can be shared with your care team.

If you notice any new symptoms after a study, contact your primary clinician promptly.
