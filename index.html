What this service provides

Specialist interpretation of cardiac imaging studies with structured reporting, clinical communication, and second-opinion support. Designed for hospitals and clinics needing expert review and clarity — and for patients who require an expert second opinion on existing imaging.

How it works

A clear pathway designed for clinical reliability and professional communication.

1) Submit Clinician referral or patient second-opinion request with the clinical question.
2) Screening Confirm required inputs and clarify scope before interpretation begins.
3) Review Consultant interpretation focused on answering the clinical question.
4) Report Structured report delivered through agreed communication pathway.
5) Clarify Follow-up clarification for referrers when needed (non-emergency).
