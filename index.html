Core services

Modalities aligned to the clinical question

Every referral begins with protocol planning so the selected study addresses the exact diagnostic need.

  • EC

    Echocardiography

    Structured transthoracic and focused echo pathways with consistent measurement standards.

  • CT

    Cardiac CT

    Coronary and structural protocols selected with attention to image quality and dose context.

  • MR

    Cardiac MRI

    Tissue characterization and functional review with indication-led sequence planning.

  • ST

    Stress imaging

    Stress studies matched to patient context and interpreted alongside baseline function.

  • RV

    Second review

    Independent imaging review for nuanced questions or treatment planning discussions.

  • PL

    Care-plan support

    Interpretive summaries shared in plain language for patient and clinician alignment.
