Services

Imaging tailored to the clinical question

Each study is selected and planned to answer the specific concern, with close attention to safety, clarity, and timely reporting.

Modality focus

  • EchocardiographyComprehensive transthoracic and focused bedside assessments.
  • Cardiac CTCoronary and structural protocols with careful dose consideration.
  • Cardiac MRITissue characterization, cardiomyopathy assessment, and functional analysis.
  • Stress imagingPhysiologic and pharmacologic stress options matched to the patient.

How studies are planned

Referral review

Requests are reviewed with the clinical context to confirm the most appropriate modality and protocol.

Patient preparation

Preparation instructions are provided clearly, including medication considerations and fasting when applicable.

Acquisition

Studies follow evidence-informed protocols with adjustments based on patient comfort and diagnostic goals.

Reporting

Reports emphasize concise impressions, relevant measurements, and direct recommendations for follow-up actions.

Consultation and second review

Image review sessions can be scheduled to discuss findings with patients or referring clinicians.

Secure sharing of prior studies is supported to provide comparative insight where available.

Quality and safety

  • Protocol disciplineStandardized approaches with individualized adjustments.
  • Radiation awarenessDose considerations integrated into CT planning.
  • Comfort-firstClear communication to reduce anxiety during imaging.
