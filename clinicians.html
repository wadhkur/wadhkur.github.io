Clinician pathway

Referral, reporting & communication

A structured pathway for consultant interpretation and reporting of cardiac imaging studies. Designed to deliver clarity, professional governance, and reliable communication back to the referrer.

Not for emergency referrals. In urgent situations use local emergency pathways.

Secure handling

Share studies via agreed secure routes (details configured later).

Structured report

Clear impression aligned to the referral question.

What to include in every referral

Clinical question

State the decision you need support with. Avoid vague “review please”.

Relevant context

Key history, current symptoms (if relevant), and key prior results.

Complete study package

Images + series/sequences required to answer the question reliably.

Referral checklist

  • Patient identifier (per your governance) + DOB
  • Clinical question + key context
  • Study access method (secure route agreed)
  • Preferred return route for the report

Referral form (professional UI)

This form is designed for structured enquiries. Configure delivery later (Formspree or secure email workflow).

Secure transfer (configured by agreement)

After referral submission, agree the secure route for study access/transfer. This site does not yet claim a specific platform.

DICOM study access, structured report PDF, prior imaging reports (where relevant).
