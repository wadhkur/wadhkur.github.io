About the practice

A single-doctor clinic focused on clarity and care

The practice is centered on attentive cardiac imaging, transparent communication, and collaboration with patients and their clinicians.

Approach to care

  • Patient-centeredTime is reserved to explain studies and answer questions.
  • PrecisionConsistent protocols aim for reliable, reproducible results.
  • CollaborationCoordination with referring clinicians to align next steps.

Practice values

Every study is conducted with respect for the patient experience and the clinical context that prompted the referral.

Careful documentation and direct communication help keep decisions aligned with the broader care plan.

Facility and equipment

Imaging is performed in an environment designed for comfort and clarity, with protocols calibrated for cardiac assessment.

Equipment and techniques are selected to support high-quality cardiac imaging and consistent follow-up.
